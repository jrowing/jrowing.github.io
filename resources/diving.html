Where to dive on Praslin

Praslin's waters are exceptional — around 17 sites, rarely deeper than 20m, teeming with reef sharks, turtles, rays and clouds of colourful fish among granite boulders. Both operators below are based on Anse Volbert on the Côte d'Or.

⭐ Highly recommended White Tip Divers Anse Volbert, Praslin

A multilingual team with 10+ years diving the waters around Praslin — some of the most biodiverse in the archipelago. They tailor every dive to your experience level, across a wide range of marine environments. Genuinely hospitable, professional, and great fun. Fun dives, PADI courses, and unique sites including some they're still exploring.
