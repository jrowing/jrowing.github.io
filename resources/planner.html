Features

📝
Markdown-first

Write lesson notes, reminders and plans in plain Markdown. Full formatting — headings, bold, lists, code blocks.

🌙
Dark mode

Follows your system preference automatically. Easy on the eyes for late-night planning sessions.

📅
Calendar view

Monthly calendar with visual indicators for days that have entries. Navigate by month, jump to today.

💾
Stays in your browser

All data saved locally — no account, no cloud, no tracking. Your plans stay on your device.

🔍
Search & filter

Quickly find any note or lesson plan across all your entries.

📤
Export & import

Export all your data as JSON for backup or import into a new device. Your data, your format.

Free & open source

MIT licensed. Use it, fork it, improve it.
