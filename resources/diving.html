Diving in the Seychelles

The Seychelles sit at the confluence of several major ocean currents, making the marine life exceptionally diverse. Favourite sites include the coral gardens around Sainte Anne Marine National Park, the granite boulders of Beau Vallon, and the remote seamounts of the outer islands.
