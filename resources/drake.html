Drake Equation

The Drake Equation is a probabilistic formula proposed by astronomer Frank Drake in 1961 to estimate the number of active, communicative extraterrestrial civilizations in the Milky Way galaxy.

Equation Overview

N = R* × fp × ne × fl × fi × fc × L

Each variable represents a stage in the development of intelligent and detectable civilizations. Since many of these values remain uncertain, the Drake Equation is used as both a scientific framework and a thought experiment about life in the universe.

What it Estimates

The number of civilizations capable of communication in our galaxy.

Why it Matters

It combines astronomy, biology, and sociology into one equation.
