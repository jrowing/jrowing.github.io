Markdown Teacher Planner

Open source · For teachers

A distraction-free planning tool built for teachers. Write your lessons, mark your days, and keep notes in plain Markdown — no accounts, no cloud, no faff.

Try it in browser View on GitHub ↗
Features
  • —
    Markdown-first
    Write lesson notes, reminders and plans in plain Markdown. Headings, bold, lists, code blocks — the full format.
  • —
    Calendar view
    Monthly calendar with visual indicators for days that have entries. Navigate by month, jump to today.
  • —
    Stays in your browser
    All data saved locally — no account, no cloud, no tracking. Your plans stay on your device.
  • —
    Dark mode
    Follows your system preference automatically. Easy on the eyes for late-night planning sessions.
  • —
    Export & import
    Export all your data as JSON for backup, or import onto a new device. Your data, your format.
