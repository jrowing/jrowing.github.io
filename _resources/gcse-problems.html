52 Weekly Science Problems

A year's worth of GCSE science challenge problems — one per week, spanning physics, biology, and chemistry. Designed to make you think, not just calculate.

These problems sit above standard GCSE difficulty but below A-level — they reward careful reading, estimation, and logical argument as much as correct formula use. Each has three or four parts, and most have a should you be worried? or what does this tell us? flavour that rewards genuine engagement.

Download the problem sheet for students and the solutions separately. Full working is shown for every part.
